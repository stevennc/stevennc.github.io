Tiffany & Co.

Nike - A Legendary Pair

Nike and Tiffany & Co. came together to bookend a year-long celebration of the Air Force 1's 40th anniversary. In partnership with the Tiffany & Co. creative team, we shot timeless hero photography with Carmelo Anthony and captured beautiful and elevated footwear imagery in the style of the Tiffany world. Nike + Tiffany & Co. = A Legendary Pair.
