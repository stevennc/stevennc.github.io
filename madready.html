MAD READY PACK

NIKE

Precision on Point

The Mad Ready mindset comes from an obsession and all-consuming love for football. It is the result of an athlete's constant commitment to be better, faster, to play with more finesse & be more precise. The scope of this project included digital executions featuring two athletes, Erling Haaland and Deyna Castellanos, with the addition of a Kids storyline to promote the Mad Ready Pack's youth sizes.

MY RESPONSIBILITIES

Digital Design

TOOLS USED

Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premier Pro, Keynote


Lead digital designer for the Mad Ready Pack's digital journey across Nike's digital platforms. My responsibilities included digital design, graphic design, photo and video editing, cropping guidelines, layout design and hi-fidelity mockups.

This work can be found on nike.com, Nike App and @nike and @nikefootball's Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X and TikTok.
