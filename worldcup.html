United Pack

NIKE

DNA Reimagined

Alongside the start of the FIFA Women’s World Cup, Nike has launched their United Pack – a new colorway with four unique boots and new innovations appearing on the Phantom Luna and Tiempo 10. In collaboration with four global football stars, we reimagined Nike’s brand voice, narrative, and art direction through her definition of victory.

MY RESPONSIBILITIES

Digital Design

TOOLS USED

Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Keynote


My contributions included graphic and digital design for @nike and @nikefootball social channels, Nike App experiences, enhanced product detail pages, landing pages, email, and a soccer footwear guide on nike.com.
