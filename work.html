Work

NOCTA Basketball - Campaign Launch (2022)
SOMETIMES YOU. SOMETIMES ME. ALWAYS US. As an introduction of Drake’s first NOCTA Basketball line, our team approached the campaign with a new level of authentic videography. Partnering with skateboard videographers and the Nike Elite Youth Basketball League (EYBL), we’ve helped differentiate Drake’s unique NOCTA brand against Nike’s more traditional basketball campaigns and products.

Art Direction: Michael Spoljaric, Jake Gevalt
Videographers: Johnny Wilson, Kaylanne Diaz
Digital Design: Steven Oey

Nike x Jacquemus - Explore Page (2022)
Fashion forward designer, Simon Porte Jacquemus, shot his first Nike collaboration in the Camargue salt mountains in the South of France. To heighten the campaign’s storytelling and concept of ‘outdoor mixed with sport’, I designed an explore page that played the role of a shoppable lookbook.

Art Direction: Michael Spoljaric, Jake Gevalt
Photographer: Zoë Ghertner
Digital Design: Steven Oey

Tokyo Olympics - Global Campaign (2021)
With the 2020 Tokyo Olympics postponed to 2021, I had the opportunity to partner with the Nike Japan Studio for their digital executions ranging from Nike.com landing pages, Nike App experiences, email, and marketing deliverables. The campaign involved staggered launches from multiple Nike Brand Partners with tailored creative from the Japan Studio and brand partners.

Creative/Art Direction: Danny Demers, Everett Vangsnes
Brand Partners: sacai, Off-White™, AMBUSH®, UNDERCOVER
Digital Design: Steven Oey

ESC - Every Stitch Considered (2021)
A focus on data, style, function, and craftsmanship is at the core of Every Stitch Considered collections. Every detail, from textile and composition to the pattern, design and color, is obsessed to perfection. The result is an ultra-premium line that at once celebrates form and delivers optimal functionality.

Art Direction: Everett Vangsnes, Gabriel Abascal
Photographer: Arnaud Lajeunie
Stylist: Georgia Pendlebury
Digital Design: Steven Oey

Revinia - Storefront Designs (2020)
Designed the UI and interactions for a new and emerging brand within the skincare industry. Responsible for the eCommerce storefront, I collaborated with an art director to align the visual direction of the site with the developing packaging and marketing designs.

Art Direction: Courtney Wall
UX/UI Design: Steven Oey

Purium - Visual Redesign (2019)
A new visual design for Purium’s eCommerce store for their annual partners and members convention. Redesigned key pages with the goals of product discovery and ingredient education.

Art Direction & Design: Steven Oey

Thrive Causemetics - ADA Compliance (2020)
Provided a visual update to focus indicators and keyboard tabbing. Design considerations with WCAG 2.0 references: 1.3.1 Info and Relationships, 1.3.2 Meaningful Sequence, 2.4.1 Bypass Blocks, 2.4.3 Focus Order, & 2.4.7 Focus Visible.

Slidebelts - Homepage & PDP (2019)
Redesigned homepage and PDP highlighting their flagship product: The Survival Belt 2.0. I included custom iconography and focused on interactive sections where users could explore the features.

Black Halo - About Us (2020)

Art Direction: Jeremy VanDerpluym
UX/UI Design: Steven Oey
